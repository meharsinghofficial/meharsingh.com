AI Rank: a free "SEMrush for AI search" visibility checker

SEO has rank trackers. AI search had nothing a small brand could use for free. AI Rank shows a brand how visible it is in AI assistants — instantly, from one input field.

TL;DR

A free AI-visibility checker built as the top of a two-product funnel: AI Rank demonstrates the problem in 30 seconds; ShelfSpace sells the $497 fix. Classic product-led growth applied to a brand-new category.

The insight

Every established marketing channel has a free "check your score" tool that drives the category: website graders, SEO audits, page-speed tests. They work because they make an invisible problem visible and personal. AI search visibility had no equivalent — so I built it.

Product decisions

  • One input, instant value: enter a brand, get a visibility picture. No signup wall before the "aha" moment — friction before value kills free tools.
  • Competitor framing: results are most motivating when a competitor scores higher. The output is designed to create urgency, not just information.
  • Free by design: the tool is the marketing. Its job is to build awareness, earn shares, and hand warm, problem-aware traffic to the paid audit.
  • Cost engineering: the production version runs within Vercel's free-tier execution limits — a real constraint I designed around by trimming the analysis pipeline for the live deployment.

Positioning

"SEMrush for AI search" is deliberate: it borrows the mental model of a tool marketers already trust, so the pitch lands in five words. Naming the category clearly matters more than explaining the technology.

Results

  • Live, working product at ai-rank-azure.vercel.app.
  • Functioning free → paid funnel with ShelfSpace as the monetization layer.
